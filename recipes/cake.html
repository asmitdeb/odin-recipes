Ingredients

  • Flour
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • water
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients, then stir in the wet ingredients until a dough forms.
  2. Turn out the dough and knead.
  3. Shape the dough into a ball and let rise.
  4. Roll the dough out into a circle.
